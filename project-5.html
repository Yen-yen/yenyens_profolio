Escape Rubik’s Star

A 3D role playing puzzle game with a solvable Rubik’s cube created by Unity game engine using C#.

Live Link

Project Overview

"Rubik's Star" is a unique 3D puzzle game that combines elements of RPG and puzzle-solving. In this game, players encounter various challenges and must find suitable solutions within the scenes to guide the main character through each level. The distinctive feature of the game lies in its magical cube environments, where players need to demonstrate familiarity with manipulating magical cubes and adapt to spatial transformations.

  • Magic Cube Planet
  • Players can only move within a single plane, and to navigate to other surfaces, they must utilize the rotation of individual layers of the magical cube.
  • Diverse Mission Levels
  • The game offers more than just collecting materials. Players will engage in a variety of mini-games and tasks as they progress through the levels.
  • Increasing Difficulty
  • The difficulty of mission levels escalates, challenging players to think creatively and strategically to overcome obstacles.
  • Expanded Scenes
  • As players advance, the final levels are not limited to a single planet, introducing satellite scenes that expand the gaming experience.

"Rubik's Star" provides a captivating blend of puzzle-solving and RPG elements, offering players a unique and challenging gaming experience in a world shaped by magical cubes.
