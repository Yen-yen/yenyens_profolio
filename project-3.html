Basketball Team Management System

It streamlined basketball court reservations, saving time and enhancing communication between players and captain.

Live Link

Project Overview

Over a span of two years, our React website, crafted with 2,000 lines of JavaScript, has been a game-changer for our basketball team captain.

Automated Court Reservations: The bug within our school system exposed a security flaw, granting us access to private data embedded in the front end of the web programmer's code. This unintended access allowed us to observe how other teams allocated their chances for reserving basketball courts by inspecting the HTML code. Seizing this opportunity, our system strategically exploited the vulnerability to gain insights into the reservation strategies of other teams. This tactical approach enhanced our automated reservation feature, streamlining the process by automating the distribution of chances for basketball court rentals. This not only alleviated challenges posed by the school's complex UI but also significantly improved our chances of securing the desired courts, thereby augmenting the overall effectiveness of our reservation system.

Effortless Attendance Tracking: The platform streamlined communication by sending automated emails to players after the school system's announcement, prompting them to fill out attendance details. Reminders, including practice details, were sent a day before, eliminating the need for a shared Google sheet.

Game Video Management: The website introduced a user-friendly feature for uploading and organizing game videos, eliminating the need for a shared document and making it easy for players and friends to categorize videos by dates.
